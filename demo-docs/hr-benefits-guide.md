# TechCorp Employee Benefits Guide

## Health Insurance

### Medical Coverage
TechCorp offers three health insurance plans through BlueCross BlueShield:
- **Gold PPO**: $0 employee premium, $500 deductible, best for families
- **Silver PPO**: $50/month premium, $1,500 deductible, balanced option  
- **Bronze HDHP**: $0 premium, $3,000 deductible, includes HSA contribution

Company contributes 90% of premium costs. Coverage begins on your first day of employment.

### Health Savings Account (HSA)
Available with Bronze HDHP plan:
- Company contributes $1,200/year for individual, $2,400/year for family
- Pre-tax contributions up to IRS limits ($4,150 individual / $8,300 family for 2026)
- Funds roll over annually and are portable

### Dental & Vision
- Dental: Two cleanings per year covered 100%, major work covered 80%
- Vision: Annual eye exam and $200 toward frames/contacts

## Time Off

### Paid Time Off (PTO)
Unlimited PTO policy with 15-day minimum requirement. Manager approval needed for:
- More than 10 consecutive days
- During team's peak season (varies by department)

### Holidays
TechCorp observes 12 company holidays plus a winter closure (Dec 24-Jan 1).

Standard holidays include:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Memorial Day
- Juneteenth
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (+ day after)
- Winter Closure Week

### Sick Leave
Separate from PTO. Use as needed for illness or medical appointments. No manager approval required for up to 3 consecutive days. Extended illness requires medical documentation.

### Parental Leave
- 16 weeks paid leave for primary caregiver
- 8 weeks paid leave for secondary caregiver
- Available for birth, adoption, or foster placement
- Can be taken within 12 months of child's arrival

## Retirement

### 401(k) Plan
- Eligible after 30 days of employment
- Company matches 100% up to 4% of salary, plus 50% of next 2% (max 5% match)
- Immediate vesting on all contributions
- Managed by Fidelity

## Additional Benefits

### Learning & Development
- $2,000 annual budget for courses, books, conferences
- LinkedIn Learning subscription included
- Quarterly lunch-and-learn sessions

### Commuter Benefits
- Pre-tax commuter benefits up to $300/month
- Free parking at office locations
- Subsidized public transit passes

### Wellness
- Monthly wellness stipend: $100 for gym, fitness classes, meditation apps
- Annual health screening with $200 reward for completion
- Mental health: 8 free counseling sessions through EAP (Employee Assistance Program)

### Equipment
- Standard laptop (MacBook or ThinkPad) provided
- $500 home office stipend for remote employees (monitor, desk, chair)
- $100/month internet reimbursement

### Equity
Eligible employees receive stock options based on role and level:
- 4-year vesting schedule (1-year cliff, monthly thereafter)
- Grants reviewed annually during performance review
- Strike price set at fair market value on grant date

## Life Insurance
- Company-provided life insurance: 1x annual salary (up to $250,000)
- Optional supplemental coverage available at employee cost

## Disability Insurance
- Short-term disability: 60% salary for up to 90 days
- Long-term disability: 60% salary after 90 days, company-paid

## Enrollment

### New Hire Enrollment
You have 30 days from start date to enroll in benefits. Elections cannot be changed until next open enrollment unless you have a qualifying life event.

### Open Enrollment
Annual open enrollment occurs in November for coverage starting January 1. Notification sent via email and Workday.

### Qualifying Life Events
You can change benefits mid-year for:
- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of child
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in employment status

Contact HR at benefits@techcorp.com within 30 days of the event.
