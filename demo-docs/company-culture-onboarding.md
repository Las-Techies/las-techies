# Welcome to TechCorp - Company Culture & Values

## Our Mission
At TechCorp, we strive to innovate and create technology solutions that make people's lives easier. Our mission is to build accessible, user-friendly products that solve real-world problems.

## Core Values

### 1. Customer First
Everything we do starts with understanding our customers' needs. We actively listen to feedback and iterate quickly to deliver value. Each team member is empowered to make decisions that benefit our users.

### 2. Continuous Learning
We believe in growth mindset. TechCorp provides $2,000 annual learning budget for courses, conferences, and books. We encourage team members to dedicate 10% of their time to learning new skills.

### 3. Transparency
We operate with radical transparency. All company metrics, financials, and strategic decisions are shared in our monthly all-hands meetings. We believe informed employees make better decisions.

### 4. Work-Life Balance
We offer flexible work hours and unlimited PTO (with a minimum of 15 days required). Our core collaboration hours are 10am-3pm local time, allowing for personal scheduling around these windows.

## Communication Norms

- **Slack**: Primary tool for quick questions and team coordination. Response expected within 4 hours during work hours.
- **Email**: Used for external communication and formal documentation.
- **Video Calls**: Default to camera-on for team meetings to build connection.
- **Async First**: Document decisions in writing; not everyone needs to be in every meeting.

## Decision Making
We follow the RAPID framework:
- **Recommend**: Anyone can recommend a decision
- **Agree**: Stakeholders must agree
- **Perform**: Assigned owner executes
- **Input**: Gather from affected parties
- **Decide**: Final decision maker (usually team lead)

## Diversity & Inclusion
TechCorp is committed to building a diverse team. We have employee resource groups (ERGs) for various communities and allies. All team members complete unconscious bias training within their first month.

## Performance Reviews
Reviews happen twice yearly (June and December). We use 360-degree feedback including peer, manager, and self-assessments. Growth conversations happen monthly with your manager.
